NOTES

  • George Sziklai, a Hungarian born in 1909, invented a configuration of standard BJT transistors. This configuration was named the Sziklai pair, although you might see it referred to as the complmentary feedback pair in modern days.
  • The Sziklai Advantage
    • Negative feedback is included inherently within the transistor design, protecting from thermal runaway, and reducing distortion.
